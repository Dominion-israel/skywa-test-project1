About us


at skywa, we are efficiency experts on a mission to help businesses increase profits by eliminating lines, waiting rooms and optimising their customer's check and check out processes.




At Skywa we think writing in lines or uncomfortable writing rooms is archaic. it is not healthy and is a waste of one of our most valuable resources 'time'. Technology has solved so many other challenges, Yet, businesses still lose money while forcing customers to wait to stand on dots or sit in crowded uncomfortable waiting rooms.


At Skywa, we are technologists bringing expertise in planning, scheduling, machine learning and mobile technology to deliver an intelligent cloud customer engagement platform. The real power lies in our keep it simple design approach that allows you to set up Skywa and improve your business results in minutes with no training needed.


At Skywa, we are passionate about changing the way thw world waits.
